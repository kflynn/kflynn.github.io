Most of what you'll find here right now was produced by Flynn, who's a
tech evangelist at Buoyant, educating developers about Linkerd,
Kubernetes, and cloud-native development in general. He has spent
40+ years in software engineering (from the kernel up through
distributed applications, with a common thread of communications
and security throughout), and is a coauthor of Linkerd: Up and
Running from O'Reilly, a co-lead of the Gateway API GAMMA
initiative, and the original author and longest-serving maintainer
of the Emissary-ingress API gateway. He is most easily found as
@flynn on the CNCF or Linkerd Slacks.
