So far, what you'll find here is produced by Flynn, who was a deep
generalist before the term existed and has worked in computing since
1983. He's pretty much run the gamut from the kernel up through
distributed applications (with a common thread of communications and
security throughout) as well as working roles from solo coder up through
Silicon Valley VP.

Since 2022, he's been a tech evangelist at Buoyant, educating developers
about Linkerd, Kubernetes, and cloud-native development in general. He's
a coauthor of [Linkerd: Up and Running] from O'Reilly, a chair of the
CNCF AI Gateway Working Group, a co-lead of Gateway API for mesh, and the
original author and longest-serving maintainer of the Emissary-ingress
API gateway.

He is most easily found as @flynn on the CNCF or Linkerd Slacks.

[Linkerd: Up and Running]: https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/linkerd-up-and/9781098142308/
